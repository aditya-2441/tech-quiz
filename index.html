Tech Quiz – Rules

Round 1: MCQ Round

  • 20 Multiple Choice Questions
  • Time Limit: 20 minutes
  • Scoring: +1 for each correct, 0 for incorrect (no negative marking)
  • Submission Rule: No answers after 20 minutes
  • Tie-breaker: Earlier submission wins
  • Selection: Top 8 move to Round 2

Round 2: Pseudocode Round

  • Problem-based pseudocode questions
  • Time Limit: 15 minutes
  • Scoring: +2 for correct solution, 0 for incorrect
  • Submission Rule: No answers after 15 minutes
  • Tie-breaker: Earlier submission wins
  • Selection: Top 4 move to Round 3

Round 3: Rapid Fire Round

  • Buzzer-based questions
  • Scoring: +3 for correct, –1.5 for incorrect
  • Rule: Participant who presses buzzer first answers, then next question
  • Winner: Highest total score
